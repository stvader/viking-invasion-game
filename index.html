About game

It was the age of glorious robbers that came across the seas for winning fame and acquiring wealth. It was the age of fearless pagan warriors that killed chrisians in the names of their gods Odin and Thor.

Yes... It was the viking age!

You can be in a role of viking. You can try to win fame and try luck.

But remember that chrisians don't want to give away their lifes and treasures free. And they are very dangerous. You have your favorite viking's weapon - axes. Throw them to christans and they can't disturb you.

Good luck!
